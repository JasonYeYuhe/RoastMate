Built private. By design.

All AI generation runs on your device using Apple's Foundation Models framework. No OpenAI, no Anthropic, no Google, no analytics SDK. Your situations don't leave your iPhone, iPad, Mac, or Apple Watch.

Sign in with Apple is optional; iCloud sync (when you enable it) goes through your private CloudKit database — Apple's standard, end-to-end-encrypted account-bound storage. We don't operate a server. Verify by enabling Airplane Mode — generation still works.
