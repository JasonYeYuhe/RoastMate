Private by default.

Calm, Sharp, Savage and the "Make it sendable" rewrite run entirely on your device using Apple's Foundation Models framework — that text never leaves your iPhone, iPad, Mac, or Apple Watch, and works in Airplane Mode.

Vent and Feral private drafts route through a developer-owned cloud proxy by default (Apple's on-device model is too gentle for real venting). Our proxy doesn't keep a copy of your text; you can switch Vent / Feral to local in Settings → AI & Privacy. No analytics SDK, no advertising IDs. Sign in with Apple is optional.
